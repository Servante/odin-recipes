Bilbo's Seed Cakes

Description

Based on an old recipe from 1861, it could've been the one Tolkien was thinking of while writing the hobbit. A simple mix of vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg and caraway seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup brandy
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 2/3 cup cake flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons caraway seeds

for the glaze:

  • 1 tablespoon brandy
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • a pinch of cinnamon
  • More seeds to top

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375ºF (350ºF for one large cake).
  2. Grease and flour pans (6 mini bundts, or probably one 8-inch cake pan).
  3. Beat the butter and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add one egg at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Add the milk, brandy, and vanilla and about 1/3 cup of flour and beat for about five minutes.
  6. Sift in the rest of the flour, salt, baking powder, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Beat just until blended.
  7. Add the seeds and mix just until combined.
  8. Divide into cake pans (it makes about 3 cups of batter).
  9. Bake for about 15 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  10. Combine glace ingredients and drizzle over cooled cakes.
